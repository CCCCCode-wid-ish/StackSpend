## FAQ

**What does StackSpend actually audit?**  
StackSpend reviews the AI tools your team is paying for, the plan level, seat counts, and the monthly spend you report. It then checks for cases like overbuying team tiers, paying for unused seats, or keeping multiple tools that solve the same job.

**Is this just another generic AI recommendation tool?**  
No. The audit logic is rule-based on purpose. The product is supposed to produce recommendations a finance person can read and defend, not vague "switch to this because it feels cheaper" suggestions.

**Do I need to connect billing accounts?**  
No. The MVP works from the inputs you provide manually, which keeps the audit fast and low-friction. A later version could add direct integrations for deeper accuracy.

**What if the audit shows I am already spending well?**  
Then the product should say that clearly. The goal is not to force savings where there are none. Honest low-savings outputs build more trust than exaggerated ones.

**Who is this best for?**  
Teams that adopted AI tools quickly and now want to understand whether their current mix of ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Copilot, API usage, and related spend still makes sense for their actual workflow.
